# Do Not Track Policy for cerrotorre.dev
# https://www.eff.org/dnt-policy

This site respects Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings.

When DNT is enabled:
- No analytics tracking (even with consent)
- No personalization cookies
- No third-party tracking
- Essential cookies only

To enable DNT:
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Send "Do Not Track"
- Firefox: Preferences > Privacy > Tell sites not to track
- Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Ask websites not to track
